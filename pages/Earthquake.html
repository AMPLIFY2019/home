APEC-Taytay practices Earthquake Drill

Charis Gabrielle Gonzales Oct 9, 2019 Article

APEC Schools- Taytay practiced earthquake and fire drill, August 2019 for both AM and PM classes. The students did "duck, cover, and hold". The objective of the drill is to prepare students whenever a fire or an earthquake occurs.

Students are aware of the earthquake and they are practiced on what to do before and after the earthquake. "When you hear the bell go under the desk and get something that you can cover your head" a learning facilitator instructed. "Don't walk like a model." Ms. Rizza, the School Head mentioned to everyone.

Students brought their emergency kits while marching down the stairs with their heads covered. They also had flags with color codes- green is for complete class and red is for incomplete class.

After some reminders that Miss Rizza told them, they kept in their minds that they have to be prepared all the time and follow instructions. After the evaluation of the drill, the students went back to their classrooms and continued their lessons.
